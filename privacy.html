Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2025

Overview

Colorbolt is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) is handled when you use the Colorbolt application ("the App").

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking is implemented
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are included in the App
  • No advertising or ad-tracking frameworks are used

2. Local Storage

The App stores your preferences locally on your device using standard platform storage (localStorage on web, UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android). This data includes:

  • Your currently selected color
  • Recent color history
  • Display preferences (instructions toggle, color code visibility, haptic feedback)
  • Premium purchase status
  • Custom saved colors (if you have purchased Colorbolt Illuminated)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. In-App Purchases

Colorbolt offers an optional in-app purchase ("Colorbolt Illuminated") processed entirely through Apple's App Store or Google Play. We do not handle, process, or store any payment information. All transaction data is managed by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Widgets and Extensions

Colorbolt includes home screen widgets (iOS and Android) and an Apple Watch companion app. These extensions read your color preferences from shared local storage on your device. No data is transmitted over the network.

5. Children's Privacy

Colorbolt does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The only external connections are:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store for processing in-app purchases
  • Google Fonts for loading the Inter typeface on the website (colorbolt.net only, not the native app)

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of data breach from our end. Your locally stored preferences are protected by your device's built-in security measures.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@colorbolt.net.
